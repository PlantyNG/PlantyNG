About me

The name's Planty, also go by many other names, is a video game designer and animator who is the creator of Narez and Triku. Not much is known about me but, I originally created a YouTube channel in May 14, 2020 which mostly featured random videos of me before going terminated on August 2021 in which I also created another channel that focused on animation videos before it get terminated.

I started game development in February 2023 with Narez and the Dark Magic, a metroidvania based on my web series Narez up until it's cancellation on June 2023 and where probably I started development of Triku on July 2023 after watching a video about an engine I would used for the game. Follow me on Itch.io and YouTube to stay up-2-date with my projects.
