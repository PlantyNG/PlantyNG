Meet Triku, a flying platform-adventure game that's made for fans of Monster World IV and Shantae. Set in an undescribed world, you must work through tasks with wide difficulty for each area and cute spooky characters to dialog to for more information about the game.

Development began in July 2023 and Triku being a ghost is a technical decision. Some of the characters in the game are named after streets, lanes, roads and avenues. During on, I removed the concept of poetry because it's likely boring and started expand on the game by creating new characters and areas.
